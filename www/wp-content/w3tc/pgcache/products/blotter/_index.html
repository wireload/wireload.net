Blotter

Unforgettable calendar.

Buy now

The future will never look the same again.

Did you ever mark something in your calendar only to forget to check the calendar?

No more. Blotter is a Mac calendar viewer which places your weekly calendar right on your desktop. Discreet and out of the way, yet only a glance away.

With your week right before you, it is fast to see if you can make a meeting on Tuesday or if you’ll be able to go home early on Friday.

You can also quickly add new events or tasks using hotkeys and Blotter's status bar menu.
