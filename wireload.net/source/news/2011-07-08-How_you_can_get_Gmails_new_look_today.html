With that said, you can actually get Gmail's new interface today.

How you can get the new interface


All you need to do is to go to 'Mail Settings' (click the icon in the upper-right hand corner), and go to 'Themes' tab. Then pick either 'Preview' or 'Preview (dense).' That's it.
